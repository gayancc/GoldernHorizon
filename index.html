01 — Who We Are

Four friends chasing the last light over Lake Ontario — turning dusk into sound, and sound into something you can stand inside.

Formed in a Kensington Market loft in 2019, Golden Horizon fuses warm analog textures with widescreen modern production. Their live shows have been called “a sunset you can hear” — equal parts intimacy and immensity.

From sold-out nights at The Danforth to festival stages across Canada, the band keeps one promise: every show ends with the room glowing. Their third record, Lakelight, arrives summer 2026.
