The band

Sri Lankan soul, built for Toronto crowds.

We started Golden Horizon in 2026 to play the music we grew up on. The songs everyone knows but won't admit they love. We treat every gig like the whole room is counting on us.

Baila that won't let you sit down. Soul and Motown for when the lights go low. Pop and covers that get strangers singing together. From living-room parties to packed pub corners across the GTA, we aim for the kind of night people still talk about the next day.

“We don't play at a room. We play with it.”
